German Time Guide

1. The "Half" Rule (Crucial!)

In English, we say "half past ten" (looking back). In German, you look forward to the next hour. Halb means you are halfway to the next hour.

Example: 10:30 is "halb elf" (halfway to eleven).

2. Casual vs. Formal

The app defaults to Casual mode on startup. Here is how they differ:

Feature Casual (12h) Formal (24h)
Structure Minutes + vor/nach + Hour Hour + "Uhr" + Minutes
Example (14:10) zehn nach zwei vierzehn Uhr zehn

3. Cardinal Numbers Only

Per your learning preferences, we do not use "Viertel" (quarters). We use exact cardinal minutes:

  • fünfzehn (15)
  • fünfundvierzig (45)

Tip: The second hand will continue to tick even after you manually adjust the time!
